Credit Coaching

"aim for higher learning.."

It’s time to get real answers to your credit questions. Creditarget’s experienced credit professionals have collaborated to make repairing your credit easier and faster than ever before. Learn and enact the meaningful, positive changes to your score step-by-step. Whether you are starting new, rebuilding previous credit history, or want to protect yourself from the growing threat of identity theft, Creditarget delivers practical advice and insight through respectful, private one-on-one coaching sessions through phone and email. Open new doors to success and healthier credit using our clear and straightforward advice. You will learn the best credit advice, tips, and blueprints to make to build and maintain responsible credit. Stop letting bad credit hinder your success and dreams. Take control of your future!

In addition to empowering you with a lifelong education in financial literacy, we’re committed to guiding you through the wide range of personal strategies, interactive tools, and additional information.

  • Basic Finances

    • - Determine what type of bank accounts fit your needs and understand the differences between primary bank account types such as money market accounts, and individual retirement accounts. Acknowledge financial challenges by calculating expenses, budgets, and pinpoint your exact income.

  • Credit Management

    • - We will help you pay on time, and prevent going over your credit. This will be accomplished by keeping track of your spending and paying what you owe. When traveling, we will assist in allowing your credit company to allow international transactions and educating you on the given currency and additional criteria for credit card acceptance in your destination country. In addition, we will assist in setting up an emergency fund.

  • Education Finances

    • - Give yourself the opportunity by applying for grants, federal student loans, or federal work-study, or scholarships with confidence. We help with the challenges that require greater planning to keep you on track during the education juggling act. A personalized budget can lead to accomplishing savings goals.

  • Home Ownership

    • - Creditarget can assist in setting aside money for ongoing home maintenance and improvements. These calculations are done by using a percentage of of the price of the home, and prioritizing what things should be repaired before more problems arise.

  • Investing

    • - Find out if you have the availability to use your increased income and credit to invest and secure a stable income on the side.

  • Retirement

    • - Your retirement income should be large enough to help meet your expense yet small enough so you won’t run out of money prematurely. Creditarget Coaches help manage your risk and understanding your needs. Learn about the risk factors of longevity, inflation, and market volatility to have a happy and healthy retirement.

  • Insurance and Protection

    • - Your home may be one of your biggest assets. Rebuilding the structure of your credit is critical to protect your home and belongings.

  • Small Businesses

    • - Use a line of credit as working capital to make it easier for you to manage your cash flow as your income or expenses fluctuate. Make the right choices to have a better idea of how you can take your business to the next level.
